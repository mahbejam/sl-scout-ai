# SL Scout — AI Market Intelligence

> Know your market before you enter it.

SL Scout is a standalone AI-powered market intelligence tool built by [SOUR LEMON](https://sourlemon.io). It analyzes product demand by city, age group, gender, brand tier, and sales channel — across 7 markets and 5 languages.

## ✨ Features

- **AI-Powered Analysis** — Intelligent demand insights based on product category detection
- **5 Languages** — English, Deutsch, فارسی, العربية, Türkçe (with full RTL support)
- **7 Markets** — Austria, Germany, DACH, UK, Middle East, Iran, International
- **Audience Filters** — Gender, Age Group (13–55+), Brand Tier, Sales Channel
- **9 Live Examples** — Pre-loaded showcase cards for instant demo
- **Zero Dependencies** — Single HTML file, no build step, no backend
- **Mobile Responsive** — Works on all screen sizes

## 🎯 How It Works

1. **Enter** your product or brand name
2. **Select** your target filters:
   - 🌍 Market / Region
   - 👤 Gender
   - 🎂 Age Group
   - ✨ Brand Tier
   - 🛒 Sales Channel
3. **Click** Analyze — results appear in seconds

Or pick one of the **9 showcase examples** for an instant demo.
